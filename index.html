Yesh Raj Singh

UI/UX Designer

I am a front-end web developer with a passion for creating beautiful and functional websites. I believe in building websites that make a difference in people's lives. Whether it's a simple brochure site or a complex web application. My goal is to deliver projects on time and on budget, while exceeding client expectations.

In my free time, I enjoy experimenting with new technologies and pushing the boundaries of what's possible with the web. My portfolio showcases a selection of my best work, and I'm always open to new opportunities and challenges.

During my preparation for the JEE exam in 2020, my journey as a web developer began. The more I practiced it, the more I gained interest in it and in my spare time, I began to learn more about developing skills. I qualified for the JEE exam in 2021 and was selected for the Civil Engineering Department of the National Institute of Technology Patna.

As a novice, I was so excited to start learning civil engineering. I thought I'd be building bridges and skyscrapers. However, when I got to my first semester I realized I was just going to be learning about soil mechanics and Hydrology and Irrigation Engineering. Wow, what a disappointment!

I never left web development and I started giving it more priority from my second semester onwards. A year later, during my third semester I was selected as a Bachelors student in Data Science and Programming by the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. In addition to mastering my development skills, it gave me the opportunity to explore new areas of study.

This is my short story. I will update it soon.....
